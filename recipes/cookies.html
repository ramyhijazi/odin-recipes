Cookies

Description

This chocolate chip cookies are nothing short of spectacular. Sure to impress your peers, these cookies are easy to make and only take a handful of ingredients. Are you ready? Lets go!

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Semi-Sweet Chooclate Chips
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients.
  3. Combine the dry and wet ingredients.
  4. Bake at 350F for 15 Minutes.
  5. Serve them warm with cold milk and enjoy!
