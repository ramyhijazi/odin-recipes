Ice Cream

Description

This simple vanilla ice cream is maybe the most ingenious recipe you'll ever see. Consisting of just a few ingredients, this ice cream can be made by anyone! This ice cream is particularly tantalizing when consumed in the summer time. Are you ready to make some delicious ice cream? I am. Let's go!

Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream
  • Whole Milk
  • Sugar
  • Sea Salt
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Pour one cup of cream into a saucepan.
  2. Over medium heat, add sugar and vanilla extract until sugar dissolves.
  3. Add the remaining milk, cream, and vanilla.
  4. Stir to combine and chill in the fridge
  5. Churn it, freeze it, then enjoy it!
