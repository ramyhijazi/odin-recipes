Cake

Description

This cake is perfect for any special occassion! Who doesn't love chocolate? This cake is sure to bring smiles to the faces of everyone at the party! Bake this easy cake today!

Ingredients

    Cake Flour
    Eggs
    Milk
    Chocolate
    Sugar
    Butter
    Frosting
    Sprinkles

Steps

    Mix the dry ingredients.
    Mix the wet ingredients.
    Combine the dry and wet ingredients.
    Bake at 400F for 20 Minutes.
Top with frosting and sprinkles and enjoy!
